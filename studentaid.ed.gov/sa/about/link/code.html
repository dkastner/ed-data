Log In to My Federal Student Aid

View your federal student loan and grant history, and find out where to make your loan payment. Please note that My Federal Student Aid may be unavailable from Saturday nights at 7 p.m. through Sunday mornings at 11 a.m. Eastern time due to scheduled maintenance.

 

Don’t have an FSA ID (username and password)—or forgot yours? Create or retrieve your FSA ID now.

 

Learn more about the FSA ID.

Access Our Other Sites

 

As we work to improve and expand what you can do here, visit our other sites:

 

FAFSA.gov
Submit a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA®).

 

StudentLoans.gov

Complete your Master Promissory Note, Loan Consolidation Application or counseling (entrance, financial awareness, and exit) or request a Direct PLUS Loan or an income–driven repayment plan.

 

Myeddebt.ed.gov

Get information about your defaulted federal education debt.
