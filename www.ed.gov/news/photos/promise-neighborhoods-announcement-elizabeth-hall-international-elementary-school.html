Promise Neighborhoods Announcement at Elizabeth Hall International Elementary School

  • Minneapolis Mayor R.T. Rybak is greeted by Elizabeth Hall International Elementary School 5th graders as he arrives for the Promise Neighborhoods grant announcement. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Minneapolis Public Schools Superintendent Bernadeia Johnson discusses impact of the Northside Achievement Zone on her schools with NAZ partners at a round table discussion before the Promise Neighborhoods grant was announced. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Eric Mahmoud, principal of Harvest Prep Charter School, discusses his school's collaboration with the Northside Achievement Zone as a partner school for the NAZ program awarded a Promise Neighborhoods grant. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Elizabeth Hall International Elementary School Principal Bennice Young and fellow Principal Eric Mahmoud of Harvest Prep School Charter School (right) listen to a round table discussion about the Northside Achievement Zone's collaboration with their schools and other partners that was awarded a Promise Neighborhood grant. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Northside Achievement Zone CEO Sondra Samuels discusses her organization's progress with North Minneapolis partners and federal, state and local leaders in a round table discussion before the Promise Neighborhoods grant announcement. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Assistant Deputy Secretary Jim Shelton announces the $28 million Promise Neighborhoods grant for the Northside Achievement Zone at Elizabeth Hall International Elementary School in Minneapolis on Dec. 19. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Minnesota Governor Mark Dayton congratulates Northside Achievement Zone CEO Sondra Samuels on her organization's efforts that led to the $28 million Promise Neighorhoods award, with Assistant Deputy Secretary Jim Shelton, U.S. Senators Amy Klobuchar and Al Franken, and MN Education Commissioner Dr. Brenda Cassellius, Minneapolis Mayor R.T. Rybak, Minneapolis Public Schools Superintendent Bernadeia Johnson, and U.S. Rep. Keith Ellison. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Northside Achievement Zone CEO Sondra Samuels demonstrates how NAZ works to cultivate a culture of achievement among North Minneapolis children with T-shirt extolling a 2036 college graduation date. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • U.S. Sen. Al Franken discusses the Northside Achievement Zone's efforts with pilot family members of its Promise Neighborhood program. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • U.S. Sen. Al Franken shares some playtime with pre-kindergartners at Elizabeth Hall International Elementary School, a partner for the Promise Neighborhoods grant awarded to the Northside Achievement Zone. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Assistant Deputy Secretary Jim Shelton goes fishing with pre-kindergartners at Elizabeth Hall International Elementary School, a partner for the Promise Neighborhoods grant awarded to the Northside Achievement Zone. Photo by Bre McGee for the U.S. Department of Education.
